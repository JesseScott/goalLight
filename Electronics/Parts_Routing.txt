

PARTS
-----

ARDUINO:	Uno
TRANSISTOR:	MP222a
RESISTOR:	2.2k
POWER:		8 x 1.5V AA Battery
LIGHT:		12V Skoda CE

ROUTING
-------

- Arduino Digital Pin Out to Resistor
- Resistor to Transistor (Base)
- Transistor (Emitter) to Power Supply (Negative)
- Power Supply (Positive) to Light Source (Positive)
- Light Source (Negative) to Transistor (Collector)
- Arduino Ground to Transistor (Emitter)
